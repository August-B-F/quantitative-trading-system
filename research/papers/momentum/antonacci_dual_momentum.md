# Dual Momentum Investing / Global Equities Momentum (GEM)

**HIGH PRIORITY**

- **Authors:** Gary Antonacci
- **Year:** 2014 (book), research papers 2011-2013
- **Source:** McGraw-Hill / SSRN
- **Links:** [Optimal Momentum](https://www.optimalmomentum.com/global-equities-momentum/)

## Core Signal / Method

DUAL momentum — combines:
1. **Relative momentum:** which asset is strongest (cross-sectional ranking)
2. **Absolute momentum:** is excess return over T-bills positive (time-series filter)

Uses 12-month lookback. Assets: S&P 500, MSCI ACWI ex-US, Barclays US Aggregate Bond.

## Reported Performance

- CAGR: 17.43% (vs. 12.34% S&P 500) over 43-year period
- Average 1.5 trades per year (extremely low turnover)
- Max drawdown significantly lower than buy-and-hold equities

## Application to 8-ETF Rotation System

DIRECTLY applicable. Rank 8 ETFs by 12-month return (relative momentum). Apply absolute momentum filter: if best ETF has negative 12-month excess return over T-bills, rotate to bonds/cash. This two-layer approach is the most practical framework for an 8-ETF system.

## Known Failure Modes

- Whipsaw during V-shaped recoveries
- Late re-entry after crashes
- Single-asset concentration when only holding "best" ETF
- Extended underperformance during trendless periods
